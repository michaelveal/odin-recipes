Roast Dinner

Description

Step-by-step guide for making a delicious roast beef dinner!


Ingredients

  • Topside of beef
  • ½ cup diced onion
  • 1 pound ground chuck 80/20
  • 28 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • ½ cup diced onion
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F.
  2. Drizzle the oil over the beef and season with the salt and pepper transfer to a roasting tin and place in oven (uncovered) for 1 hour and 15 minutes (for medium) or 1 hour and 30 minutes (for well done). Baste once, halfway through cooking.
  3. Prepare the yorkshire pudding mixture. Place the flour in a jug and make a well in the centre. Add the eggs and stir together with a balloon whisk, bringing the flour into the centre with the eggs bit-by-bit. Add in the milk and whisk again until combined. It’s fine if it’s a little bit lumpy..
